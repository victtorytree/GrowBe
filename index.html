Paradigm Shift in 3d printing
Why us?

We at Printage are trying to look at 3d printing from different sides. Cause for the average user, it has always been either something remote, or too general, or just boring. Like engine parts, all those turbines and gears. We want to bring uncommon, smart and fun ways to implement 3d trends in real life.

Materials used

We offer several materials to meet each client's needs, starting from gypsum and ABS plastics, to special flexible ones, such as TPU or RUBBER.
